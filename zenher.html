About ZenHer

Everything your body deserves to know

ZenHer is a comprehensive women's health platform that helps you understand your menstrual cycle, track symptoms, log your mood, and learn about common health conditions — all in one beautifully designed space.

Period Tracking

Log your periods, predict future cycles, and understand your unique rhythm with smart calendar views.

Mood Tracker

Check in daily with your emotions and receive personalised self-care recommendations instantly.

Symptom Logging

Record cramps, bloating, fatigue and more — and spot patterns across your cycles over time.

Cycle Insights

Beautiful charts and data about your average cycle length, phase timings, and health trends.

Health Conditions

Learn about PCOS, PCOD, endometriosis, and more — with diet guides and what to watch for.

100% Private

All your data stays on your device. We never share, sell, or access your personal health information.
